# IMPROX AI — Team Guide

## What is IMPROX AI?

IMPROX AI is our private company AI workspace. Use it to create faster ideas, captions, campaign plans, image prompts and video concepts.

## How to use

1. Open the private IMPROX AI link.
2. Enter the team access code.
3. Choose a tool:
   - Genius Pro for writing and ideas
   - Vision Studio for image/poster concepts
   - Video Studio for reels/video concepts
4. Write what you need in simple language.
5. Click **Create Now**.
6. Copy the result and edit if needed.

## Good prompt examples

```text
Write 10 Instagram captions for a luxury interior design company.
```

```text
Create a premium campaign idea for a new real estate project in Mumbai.
```

```text
Make an image prompt for a clean modern poster for a digital marketing agency.
```

```text
Create a 15-second product launch reel concept for a skincare brand.
```

## Rules

- Do not share the link or access code outside IMPROX MEDIA.
- Do not upload private client passwords or financial data.
- Check important facts before publishing.
- Use AI output as a draft, not always final truth.
